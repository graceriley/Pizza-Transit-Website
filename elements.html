What is Pizza

Pizza is flatbread usually covered in tomato sauce and cheese. Pizza can also have delicious toppings on it like meat, vegatable and different types of condiments.Pizza is baked in an oven.

What is on Pizza.

Pizza usually contains a type of sauce like tomato or barbecue, a type of cheese, and usually a cool topping like pepperoni or bacon.

Pizza is an amazing type of food. Do not argue with me on this.

Where is Pizza from.

Pizza is an Italian Dish

Pizza can be traced back to the Middle East during ancient times. Greeks, Egyptians, Armenians, Israelis, and Babylonians used mud ovens to cook flat bread. Families would eat it because it was cheap. The first pizza shop was in Naples, Italy, around 1800. Now the Italian dish has spread throughout the world.
